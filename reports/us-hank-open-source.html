Best complete candidate: Bayer–Born–Lütticke provides the clearest combination of a US-calibrated model, public data, estimation machinery, replication targets and an explicit GPL-3.0 licence. Sequence-Space Jacobian is the strongest Python foundation, but is not itself a fully estimated institutional US model.
