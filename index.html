03 — validation

Validation is a gradient, not a badge.

We report how closely each model matches an independent benchmark—and say when none exists. The five models span three verification classes; an anchored match is not the same as validation. For example, obr-macro misses by 0.15% when anchored and 5.75% free-running. Compare official benchmarks →

obr-macroPublished anchor

Tracks the March 2026 EFO to 0.15% GDP MAPE.

boe-svarPublished anchor

0.32pp RMSE; 14 of 14 outturns inside the 68% ranges.

frb-usPublished anchor

Matches current pyfrbus 1.1.1 to 1.4×10⁻⁸ on shock responses.
